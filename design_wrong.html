Sometimes you aren't entirely sure how it will look or what is involved to solve your business problem. We can produce various prototypes to assist you working out exactly how you want the software to work, rather than be tied to a fixed long term contract which may not be what you wanted.


Services Offered

There are several methods to help you get what you need, and each step can be a stand alone product - this means you dont have to commit to a large outlay only to find at the end that it is not what you needed.

Simple screenshots
