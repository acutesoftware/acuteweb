    This is a full featured contacts management system. It keeps an extensive range of details on your business contacts including phone calls made and received, what stage of the 'sell' they are at (ie they have made an enquiry, they have asked for a price, they have purchased. This allows you to target specific groups.


    Screen shot of Product


    ASCON is a business tool to manage your contact relationships and track the marketing and sales process. Up to 65 fields of information can be kept on each contact grouped into Phone numbers, Work details, Groups, Internet details, Money, History and Personal details

    Each contact can have an unlimited number of history notes attached to them and they can belong to any number of groups.

    You can track the sales process with simple checkboxes which allows you to get quick lists of contacts who have:
  • contacted you, but haven't asked for prices
  • asked for a quote but haven't ordered/purchased
  • ordered but haven't recieved your product/service
  • recieved product, but haven't paid

    With a single click you can visit the contacts website or dial their phone number. Phone calls are timed and logged to the history file.
